
NimRod- Interactive web-based Clicker Game designed to help individuals with ADHD 

Nimrod, a game designed to combine cognitive training with engaging gameplay, aiming to improve focus and concentration. By offering a more enjoyable and accessible approach, Nimrod aligns with research suggesting that game-based interventions can complement traditional treatments and improve outcomes for individuals with ADHD. By providing an engaging, game-based solution to support sustained attention and concentration, thereby offering an alternative or complement to traditional treatment methods. Nimrod represents an innovative approach by seamlessly integrating cognitive training with an engaging, interactive gaming experience. Unlike traditional treatments, Nimrod employs a dynamic difficulty progression system where enemies become progressively more challenging to defeat, ensuring continuous cognitive stimulation and sustained engagement.
